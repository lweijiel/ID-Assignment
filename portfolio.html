Leadership

Coming from Jurong Secondary School, a leadership position is not hard to obtain as there are many opportunities for everyone to have. For me, I started my leadership journey as my Class Monitor in 2013. It wasn't a big achievement but it was a small step. When I progress to secondary 2, I was selected to be the student councillor and went for my interview. To my surprise, I got it and soon after, I also obtained a simple leadership role in my CCA - Scouts which I was then promoted to be the Troop Leader of Scouts which was a tough period for me. Having to juggle between Class Monitor, Student councillor, and CCA Leader, it was a stressful year but I am glad that I had the chance to experience leadership. If it wasn't for those stressfull period, I wouldn't have grown to be the person I am today.
